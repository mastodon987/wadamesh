# beta_23 (TEST build — one user-facing note per non-blank, non-# line; # lines are section comments)

# --- Fixed ---
Bluetooth companion reliability, especially messages and repeater admin. When a phone or Mac was connected over Bluetooth, the device was sending it a log entry for every LoRa packet it heard — on a busy mesh that swamped the Bluetooth link and starved the frames that actually matter. The device no longer sends that packet log over Bluetooth (USB and Wi-Fi companions still get it), so incoming messages reach the app and repeater / room-server admin (Manage Regions, saving parameters) stops timing out and dropping the connection. (Issues #46 and #54.)

# --- Also in this test line (carried from beta_22) ---
The signal probe reads a real signal again: it sends a directed trace "ping" to your nearest repeater, which replies, instead of a zero-hop advert that nothing answers. Thanks to Tarmo for spotting it.
Contacts discovered while your phone is connected now also show on the device's own screen, not just in the phone app. (Issue #73.)
